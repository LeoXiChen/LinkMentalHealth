Most private therapists do not have direct billing with the insurance provider. So what do I have to do?

1) Check your insurance coverage, like you would for dental or chiro.
2) Attend your session, you’ll have to pay yourself
3) Get a receipt from your therapist
4) Put in a claim with your insurance provider (ie Sunlife, Manulife). Some have online claims you can use, while others require you to mail it in.
5) Get your money sent back to you within a couple weeks (sometimes sooner depending on insurance)
More information can be found here: https://www.thebalance.com/if-you-have-to-file-a-health-insurance-claim-form-2645672
