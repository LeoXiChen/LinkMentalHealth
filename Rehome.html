How It Works

STEP 1

Take Survey

Enter your postal code, click "Link Me!", and you'll be directed to a 90 second researched questionnaire to help you find the right therapist or free program for you.

Step 2

Get Matched

Choose from a short list of therapists or mental health programs that best fit your needs and your budget.

Step 3

Make Booking

Make a booking with the therapist you choose and get an appointment confirmation within 48 hours.

Why Should I Use This Platform?

You or a loved one are considering mental health support and taking a step in talking to someone. Finding the best help specific to you and your background should be stress-free. Factors like income, gender, religious preference and severity are proven to be relevant in finding the best match. By asking you a few researched questions, we use Artificial Intelligence in our matching to find and book you with the best therapist for your needs and budget. We also list the free counseling programs available in your area and display their wait times so you know where to go.

Whether you're in a tough place or just want someone to talk to, we're here to connect you with in-person therapy that you can feel comfortable with and afford.

ABOUT US

LinkMentalHeath was co-founded by two brothers who are motivated by the challenges of finding help. Our platform connects people to in-person therapy services regardless of their budget. We have a vision to make mental health support accessible to everyone who needs or wants it. We are starting with the 4.75 million Canadians who are struggling with a mental health condition but not getting the help they need. We are now launched in Mississauga and Toronto!.
