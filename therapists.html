Why did we create this platform, and why is it free for everyone to use?

Finding help should not be a superhuman feat, especially for someone seeking support.

1. 80% of people surveyed did not know their coverage with many having trouble finding it when needed.
2. Finding therapists from diverse backgrounds is hard especially under-represented communities.
3. 10% of therapists answer their phones. Emailing has been reported as a challenge by those seeking help- with a lot of back and forth to set something up.

My experiences as a person seeking support involved two months of cold calling/emailing, a 6 month wait list when I was in school, and a lot of paralyzing obstacles along the way.
